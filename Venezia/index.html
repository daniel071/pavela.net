Bigoli in salsa
Bigoli in salsa is a Venetian pasta dish including signature ingredients such as bigoli pasta and whole-wheat. This dish is easy and fast to make, making it a great mid-day dish.
2 / 3
Risotto al nero di seppia
Risotto al nero di seppia (squid ink risotto) is the definition of a Venetian dish. It comes at a price - but if you can afford it, it’s a meal of a lifetime!
3 / 3
Sarde in saor
Sarde in saor (sweet and sour sardines) is a classic dish. A couple sardines, onions and sweetened vinegar, and you’ve got a delicious dish!

Prodotti freschi

  • Seafood is very popular, and due to Venice being so close to the sea, is very accessable. - It can be found almost everywhere, primarilly the Rialto market. Some examples are squid ink and sardines.

  • Bigoli, very popular ingreadient for pasta and more. This is a locally grown wheat and serves as the core ingredient for several iconic dishes.

  • Usual fruit and vegetables are very popular, being sold in the Mestre market and more places. The Mestre market also has flowers, and specialty meat and cheese stands.
