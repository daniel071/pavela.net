Millions of people in Africa are suffering from food insecurity and poverty. If everyone in Australia just donates one or two dollars, we could raise over 30 million dollars to help those in need.

What we'll do

All of the funds raised in this campaign will go straight to the Food and Agriculture Organization's humanitarian response plan. This plan will improve food production capabilities, restore livelihoods of most vulerable populations and ensure continued accessed to information.
