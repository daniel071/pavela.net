BIDMAS Rounding Percentages

Math Lessons - Percentages

By the Moon Walkers



Learning Goal: To understand percentages and to be able to convert decimals to percentages.

Definition: Percentages are numbers but written in a different form.

It is a number expressed with a fraction of 100. For example, 60/100 would be 60%. % is the percent symbol, it is added to the end of a number to indicate it is a percentage.

    20% = 0.20 = 20/100

    60% = 0.60 = 60/100

    115% = 1.15 = 115/100

Quiz

What is 0.405 as a percentage?

To turn decimals into percentages, we have to multiply them by 100. 0.405 multiplied by 100 is 40.5, so it is written as 40.5%

What is 60/100 as a percentage?

To turn a fraction into percentages, we have to divide the numerator by the denominator. This answer would be written in decimal form (0.00), then is converted decimal form into a percentage.

60/100 = 0.6

0.6 = 0.60

0.60 = 60%

60/100 = 60%

What is 1.02 as a percentage?

To convert a decimal into a percentage, the decimal must be multiplied by 100. 1.02 multiplied by 100 is 102.
